Our Mission

We constantly hear about world hunger on the news and there are hundreds of organizations out there trying to solve this issue. But what can we do about local hunger? Hundreds of people in our own neighborhoods are hungry. These are people that have lived beside us for years, paid taxes, raised kids, and are now down on their luck. Here at Give A Meal we want to help these people in our backyards by giving them a free meal, from a local restaurant, for every $10 donated. Together, the goal is to give our neighbors some help and maybe change their luck a bit.

Timmy Rhodes San Francisco

Born and raised in up state New York Timmy went to collage studying geology however as life does happen he wasn't able to complete his program. He only arrived to San Francisco in September after a recent forest fire burned the place he was living along with all his positions. Timmy enjoys collecting gem rocks and wants to go back to school someday.

April Campbell San Francisco

An adopted child raise in Tennessee April was kicked out of her home for telling her parents she was not interested in men but women. April had to figure everything out on her own at an early age. She had a very successful career as an X-ray technician until two unexpected strokes effected her ability to control the right side of her body. With limited abilities she is still trying to live a productive life. She also has a really cute dog name rocky!

Mike & Alex Fisher San Francisco

Just engaged 9 months ago Mike and Alex seem like such a great couple. They are both working part time jobs saving their money to get an apartment. Their future goals would include starting a family together once they get their lives back on track. With our help I know we can get these guys closer to their goals.
