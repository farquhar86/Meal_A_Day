Our Mission

We hear about world hunger all the time on the news and there are hundreds of organizations out there trying to solve this issue. But what about local hunger. Hundreds of people in our neighborhoods are hungry. These are people that have lived beside us for years, paid taxes, raised kids and are now down on their luck. Here at Give A Meal we want to help these people in our backyards by giving them a free meal, from a local restaurant, for every $10 donated. Together the goal is to give our neighbors another shot and maybe change their luck a little bit.

Kevin Guntrup & Diane Ritey San Francisco

Kevin born and raised in Hawaii moved around quite a bit with his dad being in the military. After working 38 years for the same employer Kevin injured his back on the job site and on his doctors recommendation he was asked to take two months off to recover. After letting his employer know of his injury he was let go from the company and never brought back to work. Coupled with his wife Diane needing two hip surgeries they were not able to afford their current standard of living and was forced to living on the streets. Kevin is currently looking for work.

Emmitt Oliver San Francisco

You don't miss water until your well goes dry. Emmitt has only been living on the street sense the beginning of August. He spent many years in the army however decided he wanted a career change so he could be home more. He also sad he was home sick for "corn beans and rice". It is only after some legal matters his home was taken away from him.

Larry Mccail San Francisco

Always keeping busy, Larry moved to San Francisco in 2009 to help after a death in the family. He doesn't want to be on the street and is down on his luck. He knows things will change one day and has a positive attitude.
